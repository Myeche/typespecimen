The story of the typeface and its designer

  • 1967

  • Łukasz joined the design department of Gazeta Wyborcza for which he drew his first typeface.

    1990

    Łukasz Dziedzic was born in Warsaw

  • 2007

  • Dziedzic desgined the Lato Family with five weights and the accompanying italics

    2010

    Dziedzic starts publishing font for FontShop International Foundery

  • 2010

  • The Lato family has been extended to Latin, Cyrillic and Greek

    2011

    In December 2010, the Lato family was published under the open-source Open Font License by Łukasz’s foundry tyPoland
